Frequently Asked Questions

What is this project?
Web Bio Tools is an experimental collection of browser-based bioinformatics utilities written in Rust and compiled to WebAssembly.
Where are the example sequences from?
The example sequences are short fragments retrieved from public NCBI records. They are included solely for demonstration purposes.
When I save a result do I have to log back in to see it?
No. Saved results are encoded in shareable URLs, so you can revisit them without logging in.
